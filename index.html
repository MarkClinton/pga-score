Welcome to the PGA Score.

PGA Score is a card based memory game designed to test both your memory and knowledge of golf. The idea is simple, depending on your choice of course (difficulty) a number of cards will display, face down, in the game area.

Each card will have a corresponding match, match all pairs to win the game.

The K Club

This course contains 8 cards, 4 matching pairs. It has a difficulty of easy. The player must match all 4 pairs to win.

Augusta

This course contains 12 cards, 6 matching pairs. It has a difficulty of hard. The player must match all 6 pairs to win.
